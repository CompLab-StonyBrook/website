IACS Info Session

Consider attending the IACS info session for computationally minded graduate students. The session will be held this Thursday, October 11, from 12:30pm to 2:00pm in the IACS Seminar Room. All kinds of topics will be covered, but perhaps the most important one is IACS’s advanced graduate certificate in data science. This certificate is available for students in our M.A. and Ph.D. programs.
