Computational Courses in Fall 2018

The following graduate courses in computational linguistics will be offered in our department this semester:

  1. Artificial Language Learning (Michael Becker)
  2. Computational Linguistics 1 (Jiwon Yun)
  3. Computational Morphology (Jeffrey Heinz)
  4. Mathematical Methods (Thomas Graf)

There’s also several talks and workshops lined up for this semester. Keep your eyes peeled.
